Instructions

Clash of Monsters is a game centering on the battle of monsters, in which players pick their roster to battle at each phase. Players have three slots for monsters and a randomized selection of shop cards. In each phase, players make choices regarding the monsters and foods given and try to do their best to build the strongest possible roster.

Players begin with 5 HP, losing 1 HP per defeat they receive. The game is lost when a player reaches 0 HP. On the other hand, the player gains 1 victory upon winning; when the player reaches 10 victories, the player wins the game.

The shop gets 3 new monsters and 2 new foods on phase level 2/4/7; the players also receive 6/7/8 cards starting at these phases. The newer monsters not only have higher basic stats(Attack and HP) but, most importantly, more stats such as(SPEED and POWER) The Player can place duplicate monsters to increase their level, maxed at +3. The player can also increase the monster's basic stats with food; note the food invested in a monster is not refundable.

There is no time limit to the preparation phase, so take your time to plan your team, you can check the detailed stats as well as the background story of monsters with the “Detail” button.

That's it! Good luck and have fun!
